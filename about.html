About Zimtile

Zimtile formerly Marley, has been operating in Zimbabwe since 1957 and is a subsidiary of PG Industries Zimbabwe. It has three manufacturing plants in Harare, Bulawayo and in Epworth. Zimtile manufactures concrete roofing tiles, common bricks, blocks and pavers as main line products. Zimtile is the leading manufacturer and distributor of concrete roofing tiles in Zimbabwe. As part of the company’s growth and expansion strategy, the company commissioned a new state of-the-art P70HD tile-making technology at its Harare Plant in 2011. The Harare Plant has a capacity of producing 40,000 tiles per 8 hour shift. The Bulawayo Tile Plant extruder was upgraded in 2010 with a capacity of producing 26,000 tiles per 8 hour shift and recently a new HB20 Brick machine has been commissioned in Bulawayo with a capacity of 20 000 units per 8 hour shift. The company has established satellite branches in Masvingo,Kwekwe, Mutare and Gweru. To increase its distribution network Zimtile has identified approved distribution agents to market its products throughout the country. Regionally, Zimtile exports mainly to Zambia, Mozambique and Democratic Republic of Congo.

Zimtile is the leading manufacturers and distributors of quality concrete roof tiles, bricks, pavers and other ancillary products. The division has been awarded various international accreditations such as the ISO 9001:2015 Certification. Certification to the IS0 9001:2015 was a demonstration of the organisation’s commitment to quality and the ability to satisfy customer requirements. The maintenance of the ISO Quality Management System continues to benefit the company through increased effectiveness, efficiency and cost reduction.
