Mission Statement

We are the manufacturer and distributor of quality roof tiles, bricks, pavers and other complementary products to private and public organizations including, individuals, construction companies, property developers and to the Government of Zimbabwe and the region.

Our Vision

To be the leading quality, low cost producer and distributor of concrete roof tiles and related products in Zimbabwe and the region.

Our values

Zimtile is passionate about satisfying its customers’ needs because: Zimtile people make a difference, Zimtile helps customers succeed, Zimtile is a trusted partner, Zimtile delivers on its commitments
